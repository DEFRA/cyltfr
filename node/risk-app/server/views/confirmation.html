Things you can do

There are some actions you can take if you’re at risk of flooding. You can:

  • plan how you'll respond by preparing for flooding

  • receive a free call or message from the Environment Agency to warn you about flooding - check if your area is covered by signing up for flood warnings

  • find out more detailed information about surface water and groundwater flooding from your Lead Local Flood Authority (LLFA)

  • understand your flood risk now and in the next few days by visiting the national flood information service, monitoring river and sea levels for flooding and checking the 5-day flood risk outlook.

  • get evidence of your flood risk for your insurer by contacting the Environment Agency

  • check if your community has a flood action group

  • tell your community how to check their flood risk on Facebook or Twitter

  • find out which flood zone a location is in using the flood map for planning service
