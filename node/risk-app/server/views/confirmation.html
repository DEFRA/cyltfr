What you can do next

Based on your results we recommend that you:

  • Create a personal flood plan for a list of things you should do
  • Sign up for flood warnings by phone, email or text message
  • Monitor river and sea levels to understand your flood risk now and in the next few days
  • Read about how to prepare for flooding to get advice on protecting yourself and your property

You can also:

  • Contact your LLFA if you want more detailed information about your surface water flood risk
  • Contact the Environment Agency if your insurer asks for evidence of your flood risk
  • Join a flood action group - local volunteers who offer help and support to your community
  • Share your flood risk with your community on Facebook or Twitter
