Before you start

Find out about who is at risk from flooding and the impacts.

Who is at risk

5.2 million homes and businesses are at risk of flooding in England.

You can still suffer from flooding if you live on a hill or far from a river.

The impacts

If you are flooded:

  • £30,000 is the average cost of flood damage to a home - £82,000 for a business
  • £10,000 is the average cost of temporary accommodation
  • 5 months is the average time you are likely to be out of your home

Anybody can be affected - even if you live in a top floor flat. It can impact your:

  • health - through disease or impact on mental health
  • access to clean water and food supplies
  • gas, electricity and drainage
  • doctor’s surgery or hospital
  • transport - cars, buses and trains
  • school, place of work, community centre or place of worship
