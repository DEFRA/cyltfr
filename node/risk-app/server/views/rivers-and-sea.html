What the flood risk ratings mean

'High' means more than 3.3% chance of a flood each year.

'Medium' means between 1% and 3.3% chance of a flood each year.

'Low' means between 0.1% and 1% chance of a flood each year.

'Very low' means less than 0.1% chance of a flood each year.
